Reinforcement Learning

AI for Intelligent Traffic Control

Team project optimising traffic-light timing to give priority to public buses at a real intersection in Ingolstadt.

  • Role Team project
  • Domain Reinforcement learning
  • Site Real intersection, Ingolstadt
  • Stack SUMO · Python · Gym

The challenge

Give public buses priority at a real Ingolstadt intersection by optimising traffic-light timing, without making things worse for the rest of the traffic.

The approach

Model the intersection in SUMO, connect Python and SUMO through TraCI inside a custom Gym environment, and train reinforcement-learning agents to control the signals.

Prioritise buses

Signal timing tuned to favour public transport

Why RL

Signal timing is a sequential decision problem, so an agent can learn a control policy from interaction with the simulation.

The environment

A custom Gym wrapper lets standard RL algorithms drive SUMO directly through TraCI.

Result

A working RL pipeline that learns traffic-light policies in simulation to prioritise buses at a real intersection, and hands-on experience wiring a simulator to modern RL libraries through a custom Gym environment.
