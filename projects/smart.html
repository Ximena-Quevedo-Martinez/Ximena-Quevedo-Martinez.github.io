Electric Vehicles · Startup

Smart Internal Mobility — Electronics Lead

Led the electronics for a startup building utility electric vehicles for in-company transport of people and cargo, a fleet of seven.

  • Role Head of Electronics
  • Domain Electric vehicles · IoT
  • Fleet 7 vehicles, 700 kg payload
  • Stack PCB · MQTT · GNSS

The challenge

Build reliable utility electric vehicles for moving people and cargo inside a company site, and own the entire electronic system end to end.

The approach

Own electronics from telemetry to PCB to wiring: stable IoT telemetry, custom boards, clean vehicle wiring, EMC and grounding fixes, and a live operations dashboard.

Leadership

As Head of Electronics I hired and managed a technician and owned the electronic system.

Seven utility EVs, 7.5 to 10 kW, 700 kg payload, used for in-company transport of people and cargo.

Result

A fleet of seven working utility EVs with a stable telemetry and dashboard system, owned from PCB and wiring up to the live monitoring platform, and my first experience leading a small electronics team.
