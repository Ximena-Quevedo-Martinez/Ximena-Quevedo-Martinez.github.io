EDIDT — AI-Based Educational Diagnostics

Co-founder of an early-stage venture using AI to diagnose learning and help identify each learner's needs. Currently at demo stage.

  • Role Co-founder
  • Stage Early-stage, demo
  • Domain Education · AI
  • Focus Learning diagnostics

The challenge

Assessing what each learner actually needs is slow and hard to do at scale, so teachers rarely get an individual picture in time to act on it.

The approach

Use AI to assess the learning signals and surface each learner's specific needs, turning scattered information into one individual diagnostic.

Demo product

My role

Co-founder, building the concept and the product from scratch with a small team.

Where it stands

At demo stage, validating the approach with real users before scaling.

Result

A working demo that turns the idea of AI-based educational diagnostics into something I can show and test with users, plus first-hand experience taking a venture from concept toward product.
