About

I am a mechatronics engineer (B.Eng.) with a Master of Engineering in Automated Driving and Vehicle Safety from Technische Hochschule Ingolstadt. My work sits where software, electronics and mechanics meet: building, integrating and validating systems for vehicles and autonomous machines.

Today I work as a development engineer in ADAS Hardware-in-the-Loop testing. Along the way I have led electronics for an electric-vehicle startup, designed control for an autonomous race car, and built a cooperative-driving navigation algorithm in ROS2 and C++. I enjoy taking a product from idea to a working, tested system.

Robotics · ROS2

Cooperative-Driving Occupancy Grid (Master's Thesis)

Designed and implemented a collision-avoidance strategy for cooperative driving. A custom Nav2 costmap-layer plugin in C++ (pluginlib) marks the shared drivable area of two vehicles, computes time-to-collision regions and keeps the ego vehicle out of zones a cooperating vehicle will occupy. Built with ROS2 Humble, custom V2V messages, geometric algorithms and a full CI/CD pipeline.

  • ROS2 / Nav2
  • C++ / Python
  • V2V
  • Docker CI/CD
  • gtest
